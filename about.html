About Cat-5 Armory

Professional Firearms Manufacturing & Retail Excellence

Our Story

Cat-5 Armory LLC is a premier firearms manufacturing and retail company based in Naples, Florida. Founded on the principles of quality, integrity, and exceptional customer service, we have established ourselves as a trusted name in the firearms industry.

Our team combines decades of experience in firearms manufacturing, retail, and customer service to deliver an unmatched experience for both seasoned enthusiasts and first-time buyers. We carry an extensive selection of the industry's top brands to ensure our customers have access to the best products at competitive prices.
